## NOTES

- **Dofollow links** pass link equity (SEO juice) directly. Prioritize these for pure SEO value.
- **Nofollow links** from high-DA sites (LinkedIn DA 99, Reddit DA 97, GitHub DA 96) still matter enormously for brand signals, entity recognition, referral traffic, and link profile diversity.
- **Review sites** (G2, Capterra, Trustpilot) influence LLM training data and AI-generated answers — being listed helps with AI search visibility.
- **NSFW/adult content**: Some mainstream directories may reject NSFW-adjacent products. Focus first on AI-specific directories that have explicit NSFW categories (Toolify, AI Tool Hunt, AIxploria, AI Haven).
- **Profile completeness**: A complete, active profile with logo, description, screenshots, and real reviews carries far more weight than a bare-bones listing.
