Character.AI is the biggest name in AI chat. Millions of users, thousands of community characters, and a model that genuinely feels good to talk to. But spend five minutes on r/CharacterAI and you will find the same complaints on every thread: the filter killed my conversation, my character forgot everything again, and why can't I get images or voice?

The NSFW filter is the biggest pain point. It does not just block explicit content — it interrupts romantic conversations, flags innocuous messages, and sometimes breaks mid-sentence to deliver a canned "I can't continue this" response. Users who built deep, long-running character relationships have watched them get lobotomized overnight by filter updates they never asked for.

On top of that, Janitor AI — the platform that was the go-to Character AI alternative for the past two years — recently introduced mandatory government ID verification, pushing yet another wave of users to look for something new.

We tested every major alternative. Here are the seven that are actually worth switching to in 2026.

## Why People Leave Character.AI

Before we get into the alternatives, it helps to understand the three problems driving people away. If you only care about one of these, that changes which replacement makes the most sense for you.

**The filter.** Character.AI's content filter is aggressive and getting worse. It does not just block sexual content — it flags flirting, tension, conflict, and sometimes even emotional vulnerability. Users report that conversations that worked fine six months ago now trigger the filter constantly. There is no NSFW toggle, no adult mode, and no plan to add one. The filter applies to every conversation, every character, every user.

The real frustration is how it interrupts. It does not just decline a message — it breaks the flow mid-response, replacing your character's words with a generic refusal. You cannot work around it. You cannot rephrase. The conversation is just over until the filter decides to relax.

**Memory resets.** Character.AI frequently loses conversation context. You can spend weeks building a relationship with a character — establishing backstory, inside jokes, emotional history — and then one day the character acts like you have never met. This is not a bug users encounter occasionally. It is a constant, documented problem that the team has never fully addressed.

**Missing features.** Character.AI is text-only. No AI-generated images. No voice messages. No relationship progression system. No way to deepen a connection over time beyond just chatting. For a platform built around AI companionship, the feature set has barely evolved while competitors have added photo generation, voice calls, and persistent relationship mechanics.

### 2. CrushOn AI — Closest to Character.AI Without Filters

If what you want is literally "Character.AI but the filter is off," CrushOn AI is the closest match. The interface feels familiar — character cards, a community library, and a chat experience that mirrors C.AI's flow. The difference is that nothing is filtered from message one.

Memory is inconsistent. SpicyChat handles individual sessions reasonably well, but cross-session memory is unreliable. Characters may or may not remember previous conversations. It is text-only — no images, no voice.

**Best for:** Users who want to try uncensored AI chat without spending anything.

Janitor AI was the default recommendation for anyone leaving Character.AI for the past two years. The community character library is enormous — arguably the largest outside of C.AI itself. Janitor AI still works. The character library is still great. If you do not mind the ID verification, it remains a viable option — especially with the bring-your-own-API flexibility. But the migration away from Janitor has been significant, and the community feels like it is shrinking rather than growing.

**Best for:** Users who already have characters on Janitor and do not mind ID verification.

### 7. Perchance AI — Best Zero-Commitment Option

Perchance is the polar opposite of everything else on this list. No account. No signup. No payment. You open the page and start chatting. It is completely free, completely uncensored, and completely anonymous.

The tradeoff is everything else. Conversation quality is noticeably lower than dedicated platforms. There is no memory system at all — every session starts fresh. Character creation is basic. There are no images, no voice, no relationship features.

But if you just want to see what uncensored AI chat feels like before committing to a platform, Perchance removes every barrier. You can be chatting in under ten seconds.

**Best for:** Curious users who want to test uncensored AI chat with zero commitment.

## What About Janitor AI?

Janitor AI deserves its own section because until recently it was the default answer to "what is the best Character AI alternative?" Every Reddit thread, every forum post, every YouTube video pointed to Janitor AI as the place to go for uncensored character chat.

That changed in early 2026 when Janitor AI introduced mandatory government ID verification for NSFW content. The backlash was immediate. Users who had chosen Janitor AI specifically for its low-friction, anonymous experience suddenly needed to upload a driver's license or passport to continue using the platform the way they always had.

The reasoning was regulatory compliance — platforms hosting adult AI content are under increasing pressure to verify user ages. But the implementation alienated exactly the user base that made Janitor AI popular in the first place.

The result has been a steady migration. CrushOn AI and SpicyChat AI have both seen significant user growth since the Janitor AI change. [MyDreamGirlfriend.ai](/) has also picked up users looking for a more full-featured alternative.

Janitor AI still works, and it still has the largest community character library outside of Character.AI. If you have existing characters there and you are comfortable with the ID requirement, there is no urgent reason to leave. But if you are choosing a new platform today, the friction of ID verification makes other options more appealing — especially when platforms like CrushOn and MyDreamGirlfriend offer age verification without government documents.
