# SERP Research — MyDreamGirlfriend.ai SEO Restructure

## 1. Ranking Patterns by Query Cluster

### Head Terms ("ai girlfriend app", "ai girlfriend", "virtual girlfriend")
- **Listicle roundups dominate** — "Best AI Girlfriend Apps in 2026" format ranks #1-3 on nearly every head term
- Sites like Unite.AI, CyberLink, AIxploria, il.ly, theborderlessmind own these SERPs
- Individual app homepages (candy.ai, nomi.ai, romanticai.com) rank mid-page
- Reddit threads rank for conversational/opinion queries
- **Opportunity**: A well-optimized "Best AI Girlfriend Apps" page on our own domain can capture this traffic AND position us as #1 pick

### Commercial Investigation ("best ai girlfriend app", "candy ai alternative", "candy ai vs replika")
- Affiliate/review sites dominate: aigirlfriendscout.com, aigirlfriend.expert, aimojo.io, aijourn.com
- "Alternative" pages are a proven format — "Top 10 Candy AI Alternatives" ranks well
- Versus/comparison pages rank for brand vs brand queries
- **Opportunity**: Comparison and alternative pages are high-intent, low-competition for a first-party brand

### Informational ("what is an ai girlfriend", "how does ai girlfriend work")
- Blog posts and explainers rank: sometimes-homemade.com, quora.com, jogg.ai, rubii.ai
- These are top-of-funnel but build topical authority
- **Opportunity**: Definitional/guide content builds E-E-A-T and feeds internal links to money pages

### NSFW/Feature-Specific ("nsfw ai chatbot", "ai girlfriend roleplay", "ai girlfriend images")
- Roundup listicles again dominate (myanima.ai, il.ly, aimojo.io, hollywoodlife.com)
- Feature-specific landing pages from competitors are rare — most rely on homepage alone
- **Opportunity**: Dedicated feature pages (voice, images, roleplay, chat) are an uncontested gap

## 4. Content Gaps & Opportunities

1. **Feature-specific landing pages** — Almost no competitor has dedicated SEO pages for individual features (voice, images, roleplay, relationship stages). Everyone relies on their homepage. This is a massive gap.

2. **Relationship progression content** — MyDreamGirlfriend's 6-stage system is unique. No competitor content covers this concept well. We can own "ai girlfriend relationship stages" entirely.

3. **Character creator / customization deep-dives** — Most competitors mention customization but nobody has a dedicated page explaining depth of customization options.

4. **First-party "Best Of" roundup** — Competitors don't publish their own roundup pages. A first-party "Best AI Girlfriend Apps" page that's genuinely useful (while positioning us #1) can compete with third-party listicles.

5. **How-to / guide content** — "How to create an AI girlfriend" has informational intent and thin competition from actual platforms. Most results are from random blogs.

6. **Privacy-focused content** — Users searching for privacy/safety info about AI companions find very little quality content. We can own this with our encryption messaging.
